About us

B.R.HS & JR. COLLEGE, KATOL was established in 1945 and it is managed by the Pvt. Aided. It is located in Urban area. It is located in KATOL block of NAGPUR district of Maharashtra. The school consists of Grades from 5 to 12. The school is Co-educational and it doesn't have an attached pre-primary section. The school is N/A in nature and is not using school building as a shift-school. Marathi is the medium of instructions in this school. This school is approachable by all weather road. In this school academic session starts in April
